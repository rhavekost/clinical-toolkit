# Well-Being Screening (WHO-5)

## Description

This skill provides the WHO-5 Well-Being Index, a brief measure of positive well-being that complements symptom-focused screening.

**Permission:** WHO-5 is free of charge and does not require permission for use.

## Quick Reference

| Assessment | Items | Time | Purpose | Score Range |
|------------|-------|------|---------|-------------|
| **WHO-5** | 5 | <1 min | Well-being screening | 0-25 raw (x4 to 0-100) |

## Assessment Tool

- **WHO-5**: [assets/who-5.md](assets/who-5.md)

## Interactive Administration (Item-by-Item)

1. Read each item verbatim (past two weeks).
2. Record response from 0-5 scale.
3. Sum for raw score and multiply by 4 for 0-100 index.

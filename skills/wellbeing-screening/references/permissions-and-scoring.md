# WHO-5 Permissions and Scoring

## Permission Summary

The WHO-5 Well-Being Index is free of charge and does not require permission for use.

## Scoring Summary

- Raw score: 0-25
- Index score: raw * 4 (0-100)

## Interpretation (General)

- Lower scores indicate reduced well-being.
- Use local or setting-specific cutoffs when available.
